# Project Brief

## Goal

Build a monitoring system for Chilean monetary policy that combines macroeconomic series, engineered indicators, and a decision-oriented dashboard.

## Target audience

- Recruiters in analytics and finance
- Hiring managers in banks and consulting firms
- Research teams looking for junior analyst profiles

## Business framing

The project is designed as if it were an internal macro monitoring tool. It aims to reduce the time spent gathering scattered data and improve consistency in the assessment of the monetary stance.

## Analytical outputs

- Inflation convergence tracking
- Monetary stance evaluation
- Domestic activity assessment
- External conditions monitoring
- Short written macro summary
