# Dashboard Specification

## Main sections

### 1. Inflation panel

- Chile CPI YoY
- US CPI YoY
- Inflation 3-month average
- Target line at 3%

### 2. Monetary policy panel

- Chile TPM
- Federal Funds Rate
- Real policy rate
- Chile-US policy spread

### 3. Economic activity panel

- IMACEC proxy
- Unemployment rate
- Activity rolling average

### 4. Markets and external conditions panel

- USD/CLP
- Copper price
- Oil price
- Selected momentum metrics

## Design principle

Every panel should answer a clear question, not just display a chart. The dashboard must support quick interpretation by a non-technical stakeholder.
